Two Sparrows / spatialized audio

2025

after John Murillo; stereo mixdown for browser playback

Two Sparrows is a brief glance at loss, departure, and opacity. A single voice summons a fragile symphony of quiet and accidental sounds—whispered voices, cassette recordings, dregs of AI-generated orchestras, the world outside a closed window—sounds so quiet they break or simply fade away.
